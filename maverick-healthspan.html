A note on these projections

The "squaring the curve" concept is well-supported by research on exercise and healthspan (compression of morbidity). The specific timelines shown are estimates based on epidemiological data applied to your risk profile. Individual outcomes vary. The direction and magnitude are well-supported. The exact years carry meaningful uncertainty.
