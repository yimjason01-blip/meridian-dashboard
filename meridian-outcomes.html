A note on these numbers
These estimates are based on published epidemiological data, clinical trial results, and your specific risk factors. They are directionally accurate but not precision predictions — individual outcomes vary based on genetics, adherence, and factors we can't model. The relative rankings and magnitudes are well-supported. The exact percentages carry meaningful uncertainty. We share them because approximate answers to the right questions beat precise answers to the wrong ones.
