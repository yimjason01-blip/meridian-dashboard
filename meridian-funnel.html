🛋️ The couch line — everything below requires leaving the house

The couch divide

Everything above the line is couch-compatible — answer questions, spit in a tube, tap "allow." Everything below requires scheduling, traveling, and fasting. That's where the real cliff is.

Two biggest cliffs

Profile → Wearable (−20%) — Requires owning hardware + trusting data sharing.

Profile → First lab (−30%) — First action requiring real physical effort.

The "weekend" effect

Genetic kit, wearable setup, lab scheduling — all get the same response: "I'll do it this weekend." Average delay from kit arrival to return: 23 days. Each day of delay reduces completion by ~1%.

Year 2 is bimodal

Renewals split sharply: members who completed labs renew at ~70%. Members who never got past onboarding renew at ~25%. Engagement predicts retention better than satisfaction surveys.
