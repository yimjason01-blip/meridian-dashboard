## Recommendations

1. **Top outreach targets:** Jennifer Ramirez (1099 at Colonial Life, regional, bilingual) and Steve Skigin (Guardian Life, top performer, mid-career) are the strongest fits.

2. **Network play:** David Hoxworth has a massive network and was previously between jobs when Prosperity Life eliminated his division. Even if not recruitable himself, he'd be an excellent referral source — he literally posts about helping enrollment counselors find opportunities.

3. **Consider Sales Navigator:** LinkedIn's free search is limited for this kind of recruiting. Sales Navigator with "Open to Work" filters and broader network reach would surface significantly more candidates.

4. **Alternative channels to explore:**
   - NABIP (National Association of Benefits and Insurance Professionals) job boards
   - HVBA (Health & Voluntary Benefits Association) network
   - Colonial Life / Aflac / Unum alumni groups on LinkedIn
   - Benefits conferences (CAHIP, ITC) attendee lists
   - Indeed/ZipRecruiter for active job seekers with "voluntary benefits" keywords

5. **Passive recruiting strategy:** The best candidates in this space are employed. Consider a targeted InMail campaign to people with "voluntary benefits" + "account executive" or "sales manager" titles at carriers like Colonial Life, Aflac, Unum, and Allstate Benefits.
