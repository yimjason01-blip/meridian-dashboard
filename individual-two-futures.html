What this visual is trying to say

The upside is not cosmetic.

If the same high-risk person builds elite-for-age fitness and catches major disease earlier, the gain is not just “better labs.” It is a later first drop, fewer catastrophic branches, and more life spent physically capable.

Why the default path falls early

Bottom-quartile fitness amplifies metabolic drift and fragility.
Cardiovascular and cancer risk stay hidden until they become events.
Once the first major hit lands, the whole future path shifts downward.

What Meridian changes first

Protect the downside: find the critical risk before the event.
Lift capacity: move fitness from bottom quartile toward elite-for-age.
Preserve the band that matters: more years where life still feels like your life.
Honest claim This is not “become superhuman.” It is “stop living on the bad branch of the tree.”
Inspirational claim The same person can plausibly move from early narrowing in the 50s/60s to staying active into the 70s.
Most important design question Does this feel like a real human future or does it still feel too tidy and illustrative?
